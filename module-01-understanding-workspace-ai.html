âœï¸ Exercise 1: Workflow Mapping (10 min)

Answer these questions about your current development workflow:

  1. What takes the most time? (code reviews, debugging, documentation, testing...)
  2. Where do you context-switch most? (switching between tasks, waiting for feedback...)
  3. What's repetitive? (boilerplate code, similar bug fixes, routine configs...)
  4. Where do blockers occur? (waiting for reviews, unclear requirements, complex debugging...)
  5. What knowledge is siloed? (only one person knows the system, undocumented processes...)

âœï¸ Exercise 2: AI Opportunity Assessment (10 min)

For each of your bottlenecks from Exercise 1, consider:

  1. Can AI help with this task? (Yes / Partially / No)
  2. What's the potential time savings? (Hours per week)
  3. What's the risk level? (Low / Medium / High)
  4. What would you need to get started? (Tools, training, approval)

Prioritize: Start with high-value, low-risk opportunities.
